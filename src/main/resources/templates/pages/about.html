What is InternHub?

InternHub is a modern talent-matching tool designed to make recruiting better. It seeks to automate the San Antonio Ambassadors intern selection process, aggregating applicant responses and allowing administrators to efficiently match candidates to employers based on skills and experience. This technology improves the accuracy, cost, and speed of the hiring process for everyone involved. Our project incorporates Spring Boot with Spring Security, Thymeleaf, Hibernate, and a MySQL database.
